I’M HELENA MADRID

Hi! My name is Helena Madrid, I'm 27 years old and I'm from Monterrey.

I have an undergraduate degree in Hispanic Literature from Tecnológico de Monterrey. Right after graduation I started working in New York City for an award-winning travel website called Fathom. Needlessly to say, I had one of the most amazing experiences of my life. Starting from scratch, meeting amazing people, and having a good and interesting job, it was a dream.

But now I am back in Monterrey and ready for a new adventure: working for a company that creates new bussineses based on the internet. As this is a completely new field for me so I am taking this bootcamp to better understand the business I'm now in.

Fun fact: I have more pictures of my dogs in my phone than anything else.
